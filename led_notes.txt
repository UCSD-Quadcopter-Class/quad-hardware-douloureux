LED used: SSF-LXH305ID-TR

We decided to use this LED as it had three colors available, RED, GREEN, and YELLOW. 

We planned to use the red as front and back and GREEN and YELLOW as left and right respectively.

Datasheet for RED LED: SSF-LXH305ID-TR
Datasheet for GREEN LED: SSF-LXH305GD
Datasheet for YELLOW LED: SSF-LXH305YD

For now we have added it into the schematic and will place it in the board respective of the directions of
the plane to the direction it moves.
